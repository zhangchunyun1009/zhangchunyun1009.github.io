--- layout: single title: "Teaching" permalink: /teaching/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} ## [Natural Language Processing]({{ base_path }}/teaching/2014-spring-teaching-1) Undergraduate course, *Shandong University of Finance and Economics, School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence*
This course introduces the core methods and model architectures in natural language processing, with emphasis on representation learning, sequence modeling, transformer-based architectures, and pre-trained language models.
**Syllabus** * Introduction (20250825) * Word Vector Representation (20250901) * Sequence Modeling * Neural Language Models and RNN * Attention Mechanism and Transformer * Pre-trained Models: ELMo, BERT * Pre-trained Models: GPT, BART ## [Natural Language Processing (Graduate)]({{ base_path }}/teaching/2025-fall-graduate-nlp) Graduate course, *Shandong University of Finance and Economics, School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence*
This graduate course explores advanced topics in natural language processing, including neural sequence modeling, pre-trained language models, prompt-based methods, knowledge augmentation, and emerging language agent paradigms.
**Syllabus** * Introduction * SeqModel * LSTM * attention in NLP * BERT * NLGdecoding * PLM * PromptLearning * Adaptation * RAG * LangAgent ## [Comprehensive Practice of Natural Language Processing]({{ base_path }}/teaching/2015-spring-teaching-1) Practical course, *Shandong University of Finance and Economics, School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence*
This practice-oriented course helps students connect fundamental NLP concepts with implementation, experimentation, and end-to-end system understanding.
**Focus** * Practical implementation of NLP models * Experimental design and model evaluation * End-to-end workflow for NLP applications * Training and analysis with real-world datasets
