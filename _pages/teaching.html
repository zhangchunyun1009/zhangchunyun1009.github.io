Natural Language Processing

Undergraduate course, School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Shandong University of Finance and Economics

This course introduces the core methods and model architectures in natural language processing, with emphasis on representation learning, sequence modeling, transformer-based architectures, and pre-trained language models.

Syllabus

  • Introduction (20250825)
  • Word Vector Representation (20250901)
  • Sequence Modeling
  • Neural Language Models and RNN
  • Attention Mechanism and Transformer
  • Pre-trained Models: ELMo, BERT
  • Pre-trained Models: GPT, BART

Natural Language Processing (Graduate)

Graduate course, School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Shandong University of Finance and Economics

This graduate course explores advanced topics in natural language processing, including neural sequence modeling, pre-trained language models, prompt-based methods, knowledge augmentation, and emerging language agent paradigms.

Syllabus

  • Introduction
  • SeqModel
  • LSTM
  • Attention in NLP
  • BERT
  • NLG Decoding
  • PLM
  • Prompt Learning
  • Adaptation
  • RAG
  • LangAgent

Comprehensive Practice of Natural Language Processing

Practical course, School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Shandong University of Finance and Economics

This practice-oriented course helps students connect fundamental NLP concepts with implementation, experimentation, and end-to-end system understanding.

Focus

  • Practical implementation of NLP models
  • Experimental design and model evaluation
  • End-to-end workflow for NLP applications
  • Training and analysis with real-world datasets
