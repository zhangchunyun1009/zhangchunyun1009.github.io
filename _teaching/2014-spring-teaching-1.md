</div>

## Course Outline

* Introduction (20250825)
* Word Vector Representation (20250901)
* Sequence Modeling
* Neural Language Models and RNN
* Attention Mechanism and Transformer
* Pre-trained Models: ELMo, BERT
* Pre-trained Models: GPT, BART
